Movie Tracker

An application for movie fanatics. Search for information about an actor or a movie. The homepage shows the user the current most popular movies. To add a movie to the watchlist click the eye icon and click the watchlist button to view your current watchlist. Once a movie in the watchlist has been watched, the user simply clicks the eye icon again to remove the movie from the watchlist. Click on the movie poster for more information.
